Welcome to the Starting XI Challenge!

Dive into the excitement of football and test your ball knowledge by guessing the starting lineup for the daily featured match. Focus on the team highlighted in bold—that's your challenge. Here's how it works:

  • Guess the right letter in the exact spot, and it turns green, marking a perfect hit!
  • If your guessed letter appears in a player's name but in a different place, it will light up in orange.
  • If the letter doesnt appear in that player's name, it will show up in red.
  • You get 5 guesses for each player. If you guess every player you win the challenge for that day!

A new match is available every day. Tune in daily for a new challenge. Subscribe to our newsletter to be the first to know about new challenges!
